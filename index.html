Solar System

Explore the wonders of our solar system and learn interesting facts about each planet.

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun.

Venus

Venus has a thick, toxic atmosphere that traps heat, making it the hottest planet.

Earth

Earth is our home and the only known planet that supports life.

Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet and is home to the largest volcano in the solar system.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and is famous for its Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is well known for its stunning rings, made of ice and rock particles.

Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side, causing extreme and unusual seasons.

Neptune

Neptune is a windy, icy planet and the farthest from the Sun in our solar system.
